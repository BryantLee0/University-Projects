Yamada, Our Journey

Yamada is an American motorcycle manufacturer headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States. Founded in 1922. The company has survived numerous ownership arrangements, subsidiary arrangements, periods of poor economic health and product quality, and intense global competition to become one of the world's largest motorcycle manufacturers and an iconic brand widely known for its loyal following. Our ambition is to maintain our place as the most desirable motorcycle brand in the world. Since 1922, Yamada has defined motorcycle culture by delivering a motorcycle lifestyle with distinctive and customizable motorcycles, experiences, motorcycle accessories, riding gear and apparel.

We as a company would like to thank to those who've been through with us together on this journey, and as our 100th year celebration, we decided to further expand and improve our services.
